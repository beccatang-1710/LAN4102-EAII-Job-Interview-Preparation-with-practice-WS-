Student User Guide for AI Interview Coach (LAN4102 EAII Preparation)

Presented by Rebecca Tang

Welcome to your AI Interview Coach! This Gem is specifically customised to conduct a professional mock interview and provide structured feedback to prepare you for the coming EAII job interview task. Follow the steps below to set up and practise on your mobile phone.

欢迎使用您的 AI 面试教练！本应用专门定制用于进行专业的模拟面试，并提供结构化的反馈，帮助您为即将到来的 EAII 求职面试任务做好准备。请按照以下步骤在您的手机上设置并练习。

1📱 Install the Gemini App on Your Device

📱 在您的设备上安装 Gemini 应用

To use the live voice conversation features, you must use the official mobile app for your specific phone:

要使用实时语音对话功能，您必须使用适用于您手机的官方移动应用：

🍏 iPhone Users

🍏 iPhone 用户

  1. Open the App Store and search for Google Gemini.
    打开 App Store 并搜索 Google Gemini。
  2. Download the standalone Google Gemini app (do not use the standard Google search app).
    下载独立的 Google Gemini 应用（不要使用标准的 Google 搜索应用）。
  3. Sign in using your Google account.
    使用您的 Google 账户登录。
🤖 Android Users

🤖 Android 用户

  1. Open the Google Play Store and download the Google Gemini app.
    打开 Google Play 商店并下载 Google Gemini 应用。
  2. Sign in using your Google account.
    使用您的 Google 账户登录。

2🔗 Add the Coach to Your Account

🔗 将教练添加到您的账户

On your mobile phone, click the link below to open your coach:

在您的手机上，点击以下链接打开您的教练：

👉 Open LAN4102_EAII Job Interview Coach

👉 打开 LAN4102 EAII 求职面试教练

The link will open the Coach page in your phone's web browser.

该链接将在您手机的网络浏览器中打开教练页面。

Crucial Action: Type "Hi" and send this first message.

关键操作：输入 "Hi" 并发送此第一条消息。

⚠️ Note: Sending that first message is what permanently "saves" the shared Coach to your Gemini account. Once you do this, it will officially show up inside your mobile app!

⚠️ 注意：发送该第一条消息将把共享的教练永久"保存"到您的 Gemini 账户。一旦您这样做，它将正式显示在您的移动应用中！

3🎙️ Start Your Voice Practice

🎙️ 开始您的语音练习

📲

Open your downloaded Google Gemini Mobile App.
打开您已下载的 Google Gemini 移动应用。

☰

Tap the Menu (three horizontal lines) in the top-left corner.
点击左上角的菜单（三条横线）。

💎

Look under your list of Gems and tap LAN4102_EAII Job Interview Coach.
在您的应用列表中找到并点击 LAN4102 EAII 求职面试教练。

🔊

To Listen to Responses: Tap the Speaker icon next to any text the AI sends you.
听取回复：点击 AI 发送的文字旁边的扬声器图标。

🌊

For Live Spoken Practice (Recommended): Tap the Gemini Live waveform icon in the bottom-right corner next to the chat box. This starts a real-time, hands-free spoken conversation.
实时口语练习（推荐）：点击聊天框右下角的 Gemini Live 波形图标，开始实时免提口语对话。

🎓 Get Ready for Your Mock Interview

🎓 为您的模拟面试做好准备

📄
Clinic materials
Click to download the Word document (.docx)
点击下载 Word 文档（.docx）
⬇

Download "Clinic materials". Then, draft your answers before you start practising. The coach will start with this question: "Could you please introduce yourself in 2 minutes?" Feedback will be given afterwards. After that, the coach will pick exactly one question from each of the 4 categories below.

请先下载"Clinic materials"，然后在开始练习前列出您的回答要点。教练会以这个问题开始："请用两分钟介绍一下自己好吗？"回答后会收到反馈。之后，教练会从下方 4 个类别中各选1 个问题。

🗂️ Questions You May Be Asked 您将面对的问题

Category / 类别Sample Interview Questions / 面试问题示例
1. Strength and Weaknesses
优点与缺点
  • Can you describe a time when your strengths helped you succeed?
    您能描述一个您的优点帮助您取得成功的经历吗？
  • What is a weakness you've identified in yourself, and how are you addressing it?
    您在自己身上发现了什么缺点？您是如何改进的？
  • What are the important qualities a person should have to become an effective team member?
    要成为一名高效的团队成员，一个人应该具备哪些重要品质？
  • How do you ensure your weaknesses do not impact your work performance?
    您如何确保自己的缺点不会影响工作表现？
  • In what areas do you feel you need the most improvement?
    您觉得自己在哪些方面最需要提升？
2. Long/ Short-term Goals
长期 / 短期目标
  • What is your plan for your personal growth and development?
    您的个人成长和发展计划是什么？
  • What are your long-term career goals?
    您的长期职业目标是什么？
  • What are your goals for the next five years / ten years? How do you plan to achieve those goals?
    您未来五年/十年的目标是什么？您计划如何实现这些目标？
  • What would you like to study in the near future?
    您近期想学习什么？
  • Will you consider taking any training courses related to this field/job?
    您会考虑参加与该领域/工作相关的培训课程吗？
3. Problem-solving
解决问题
  • How do you deal with difficult customers / irresponsible colleagues?
    您如何应对难缠的客户/不负责任的同事？
  • How do you manage multiple tasks?
    您如何管理多项任务？
  • How do you handle office gossip?
    您如何处理办公室八卦？
  • Describe a difficult work situation / project and how you overcame it?
    请描述一个困难的工作情况/项目，以及您是如何克服的？
  • Can you tell us about a situation in which you were required to analyse and solve a complicated problem?
    您能讲述一个您需要分析并解决复杂问题的情况吗？
  • How do you deal with conflicts with peers?
    您如何处理与同事之间的冲突？
4. Soft Skills
软技能
  • Do you work better as a team member or alone? Why?
    您作为团队成员还是单独工作效果更好？为什么？
  • Can you provide us with an example of your ability to work independently?
    您能举一个您独立工作能力的例子吗？
  • How do you handle stress and pressure?
    您如何应对压力？
  • Do you mind traveling frequently to Mainland China?
    您是否介意经常前往中国大陆出差？
  • Are you willing to work under pressure and overtime?
    您是否愿意在压力下工作并加班？
  • Do you have any full-time / part-time work experience?
    您有全职/兼职工作经验吗？
  • What have you gained from your summer work / part-time work?
    您从暑期工作/兼职工作中获得了什么？
  • What do you know about the type of work we expect from you?
    您对我们期望您从事的工作类型了解多少？

💬 The Feedback You Will Receive 您将获得的反馈

💬 您将获得的反馈

After each of your answers, the coach will give you concise, point-by-point spoken feedback. Here's exactly what to expect:
在您每次回答后，教练会提供简洁、按要点展开的口语反馈。以下是您将获得的内容：

  1. 1. Pronunciation 发音
  2. 2. Pace & Rhythm 语速与节奏
  3. 3. Grammar 语法
  4. 4. Fluency 流利度
  5. 5. Content Tip 内容建议
  6. 6. Sample Response 范例回答
