Greeting
Good morning. Thank you for having me here today.早上好。谢谢今天邀请我来这里。
My name is [Sharon], and I'm a Higher Diploma student in [Landscape Architecture] at [HKDI].我叫 Sharon，是香港知专设计学院园境建筑专业的高级文凭学生。
I'm excited to be here to discuss my passion for [sustainable design] and how I can contribute to your team.我很高兴来到这里讨论我对可持续设计的热情，以及我如何为您的团队做出贡献。
Relevant Education / Knowledge / Skills
During my Higher Diploma studies, I excelled in courses that covered [site analysis] and [urban planning], which equipped me with essential knowledge to tackle real-world challenges.在我的高级文凭学习期间，我在涉及场地分析和城市规划的课程中表现出色，这使我具备了应对现实挑战的基本知识。
I believe my academic background has prepared me well for this role.我相信我的学术背景已经为我胜任这个职位做好了充分的准备。
Relevant Work Experience
I worked at [ABC Design Firm] last summer as an intern, where I had the opportunity to assist in [landscape mapping].去年夏天，我在 ABC 设计事务所担任实习生，在那里我有机会协助景观绘图。
This experience allowed me to apply my knowledge in a practical setting and enhanced my skills in communication / collaboration / problem-solving / [any other competencies].这次经历让我能够在实际环境中运用所学知识，并提升了我在沟通、协作和解决问题方面的能力。
Relevant Hobbies / Extracurricular Activities
In my leisure time, I enjoy [sketching nature], which helps me stay inspired and informed about the latest trends.在闲暇时间，我喜欢写生，这有助于我保持灵感并了解最新趋势。
Closing
Thank you for considering my application.感谢您考虑我的申请。
I look forward to the possibility of working together and contributing to your team.我期待着合作并为您的团队做出贡献的可能性。

Category 1 – Self-Introduction
What do you do in your spare time?你业余时间都做些什么？
I see you have done some voluntary work. What did you learn from that?我看到你做过一些义工。你从中学到了什么？
What three keywords would your peers use to describe you?你的同学/同事会用哪三个关键词来形容你？
Category 2 – Strengths & Weaknesses
What are your strengths / weaknesses?你的优缺点是什么？
What three words would you use to describe yourself?你会用哪三个词来形容自己？
Can you describe a time when your strengths helped you succeed?你能描述一次你的优势帮助你取得成功的经历吗？
What is a weakness you've identified in yourself, and how are you addressing it?你认识到自己的哪些不足，你是如何改进的？
What are the important qualities a person should have to become an effective team member?一个人需要具备哪些重要素质才能成为有效的团队成员？
How do you ensure your weaknesses do not impact your work performance?你如何确保自己的不足不会影响工作表现？
In what areas do you feel you need the most improvement?你觉得自己在哪些方面最需要改进？
Category 3 – Career Goals
What are your goals for the next 5 / 10 years? How do you plan to achieve those goals?你未来 5/10 年的目标是什么？你打算如何实现这些目标？
What are your long-term career goals?你的长期职业目标是什么？
What is your personal growth and development plan?你的个人成长和发展计划是什么？
Would you consider taking any training courses related to this field / job?你会考虑参加与该领域/工作相关的培训课程吗？
What would you like to study in the near future?近期你想学习什么？
Technical / Knowledge-Based Questions
How are your studies at IVE related to this job?你在 IVE 的学习与这份工作有什么关系？
What have you learnt at IVE to prepare you for this job?你在 IVE 学到了什么来为这份工作做准备？
What is your favourite subject at IVE? What have you learnt from it?你在 IVE 最喜欢的科目是什么？你从中学到了什么？
What skills / knowledge have you acquired from your full-time / part-time work?你从全职/兼职工作中获得了哪些技能/知识？
What have you gained from your summer work / part-time work / voluntary work?你从暑期工作/兼职工作/志愿工作中收获了什么？
Category 4 – Problem-Solving / Situational Questions
Describe a difficult work situation / project and explain how you overcame it.描述一个困难的工作情况/项目，并解释你是如何克服它的。
How would you deal with difficult customers / patients / colleagues?你会如何处理难缠的客户/患者/同事？
How would you manage multiple tasks?你会如何管理多项任务？
Can you tell us about a situation in which you were required to solve a complicated problem?你能告诉我们你被要求解决一个复杂问题的情况吗？
Describe a time when you worked well under pressure. How did you handle the situation?描述一次你在压力下表现良好的经历。你是如何处理这种情况的？
How do you handle office gossip?你如何处理办公室流言蜚语？
How do you deal with conflicts with peers?你如何处理与同事之间的冲突？
Category 5 – Soft Skills & Work Experience
Can you provide us with an example of your ability to work independently?你能给我们提供一个你独立工作能力的例子吗？
Do you work better as a team member or alone? Give an example.你作为一个团队成员还是独自工作表现更好？举个例子。
How do you handle stress and pressure?你如何应对压力？
Do you mind traveling frequently to Mainland China?你介意经常前往中国内地出差吗？
Are you willing to work under pressure and overtime?你愿意在压力下工作和加班吗？
Do you have any full-time / part-time work experience?你有全职/兼职工作经验吗？
What have you gained from your summer work / part-time work?你从暑期工作/兼职工作中收获了什么？
What do you know about the type of work we expect from you?你对我们期望你完成的工作类型了解多少？
Other Popular Questions
What do you know about our company?你对我们公司了解多少？
What interests you about this job?你对这份工作感兴趣的地方是什么？
Why do you want this job?你为什么想要这份工作？
Since you are quite fresh compared to other candidates, what makes you think you are qualified for the post?与其他候选人相比，你还很年轻/没有经验，为什么你认为自己能胜任这个职位？
What makes you think you are a suitable candidate for the post?为什么你认为自己是这个职位的合适人选？
What can you contribute to our company?你能为我们公司做出什么贡献？
Why should we hire you?我们为什么要聘用你？

🔵 Questions highlighted in blue are newly added from the clinic session materials.
