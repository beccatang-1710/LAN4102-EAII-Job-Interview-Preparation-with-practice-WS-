[Opening] 《開場》

Good morning/afternoon. It’s my pleasure to share with you my ideas on innovative marketing strategies for PCCW Media Limited in the dynamic world of social media. Today, we’ll explore the challenges and solutions to effectively promote PCCW Media in this competitive landscape. 早上好／下午好。我很高興能與大家分享我對PCCW Media Limited在動態社交媒體世界中創新行銷策略的想法。今天，我們將探討有效推廣PCCW Media的挑戰與解決方案。

[Introduction] 《簡介》

As we all know, PCCW Media Limited is a key player in Hong Kong’s media industry, offering diverse content through platforms like Now TV. As social media becomes increasingly influential, leveraging these platforms is crucial for reaching and engaging audiences. 眾所周知，PCCW Media Limited是香港媒體業的重要參與者，通過Now TV等平台提供多元化內容。隨著社交媒體影響力越來越大，利用這些平台對張達和吸引觀眾至關重要。

[Problems / Challenges] 《問題／挑戰》

One of the challenges is the intense competition from global streaming services like Netflix. Also, it is tough to maintain audience engagement amidst content saturation. The last issue is adapting to rapidly changing platform algorithms. 其中一個挑戰是來自Netflix等全球流媒體服務的激烈競爭。另外，在內容飽和的情況下維持觀眾參與度也很困難。最後一個問題是適應迅速變化的平台演算法。

[Solution 1 + Elaboration] 《解決方案一 + 闗述》

To tackle these challenges, first, I would suggest creating interactive content such as polls, quizzes, and live Q&A sessions. This would encourage active participation and build a community around PCCW Media. 為了解決這些問題，首先我會建議創建互動性內容，例如投票、籌題與即時問答會。這將鼓勵主動參與，並在PCCW Media周圍建立社群。

[Solution 2 + Elaboration] 《解決方案二 + 闗述》

Second, it is important to partner with influencers who align with PCCW Media’s values. As a result, brand visibility and credibility can be enhanced. 第二，與符合PCCW Media価值觀的署紅合作非常重要。因此，品牌曝光度和公信力可以得到提升。

[Solution 3 + Elaboration] 《解決方案三 + 闗述》

Third, it would be advantageous to use data analytics to design personalized ad campaigns so that the right message can reach the right audience at the right time. 第三，利用數據分析設計個人化廣告烏行動是有利的，這樣正確的訊息就能在適當的時間傳達給適當的觀眾。

[Summary] 《總結》

In conclusion, PCCW Media can create social media posts that grab attention by engaging audiences with interactive content, collaborating with influencers, and using targeted ads to reach specific demographics. 總而言之，PCCW Media可以通過互動內容吸引觀眾、與署紅合作，以及使用精準廣告來觸達特定人群，創造出吸引人的社交媒體帖文。

[Closing] 《結尾》

Thank you for listening. 感謝聽取。
Opening 《開場》
“Good morning / afternoon. It’s my pleasure to share with you my ideas on [topic].” 「早上好／下午好。我很高興能和大家分享我對[主題]的想法。」
“Thank you for giving me the chance to discuss [topic].” 「感謝你們給我這個機會討論[主題]。」
“Hello, everyone. I’m delighted to be here today to discuss [topic].” 「大家好。我很高興今天來到這裡討論[主題]。」
Building a Rapport 《建立聯繫》
“As we all know, …” 「眾所周知……」
“As you know, …” 「如你所知……」
“We are all aware that …” 「我們都知道……」
“As people who are passionate about [the industry], we know that …” 「作為熱蠻於[行業]的人。我們知道……」
Describing Problems / Challenges 《描述問題／挑戰》
“One of the challenges is …” 「其中一個挑戰是……」
“Another problem is …” 「另一個問題是……」
“There are multiple challenges. One of them is …” 「有多項挑戰。其中之一是……」
“… is an issue.” 「……是一個問題。」
Suggesting Solutions 《建議解決方案》
“I would recommend ...” 「我會建議……」
“To solve the problem, I suggest …” 「為了解決問題。我建議……」
“So, what are the solutions? First, …” 「那麼。解決方案是什麼？首先……」
“It is important / essential to …” 「……是很重要的／必不可少的」
“A possible option is to …” 「一個可行的選擇是……」
“There are some possible solutions to improve the situation. First, … Second, … Third, …” 「有一些可行的解決方案來改善情況。首先……其次……第三……」
“It would be advantageous / beneficial to …” 「……將會是有利的／有益的」
Giving Elaboration 《進行闗述》
“This would help …” 「這將有助於……」
“This would enable ...” 「這將能夠……」
“This could …” 「這可以……」
“As a result, ...” 「因此……」
“…, so that ...” 「……以便……」
“This may result in …” 「這可能會導致……」
“For example, ...” 「例如……」
“For instance, ...” 「舉例來說……」
“This way, …” 「這樣一來……」
Summarising / Concluding 《總結》
“To conclude / To sum up, the challenge(s) can be addressed by ...” 「總而言之。這些挑戰可以通過……來應對。」
“In short, the situation can be improved by …” 「簡而言之。可以通過……來改善情況。」
“In conclusion / In summary, the problem(s) can be tackled by …” 「總結來說。這些問題可以通過……來解決。」
“By adopting the suggestions that I have mentioned, I am confident that …” 「通過採納我提到的建議。我確信……」
Closing / Ending 《結尾》
“This brings me to the end of my reflection. Thank you.” 「我的反思到此結束。謝謝。」
“This is the end of my reflection. Thank you.” 「我的反思到此為止。謝謝。」
“That covers all I wanted to share today. Thank you.” 「以上是我今天想分享的全部內容。謝謝。」
