Relevant Education / Knowledge / Skills
During my Higher Diploma studies, I excelled in courses that covered [site analysis] and [urban planning], which equipped me with essential knowledge to tackle real-world challenges.在我的高级文凭学习期间，我在涉及场地分析和城市规划的课程中表现出色，这使我具备了应对现实挑战的基本知识。

Relevant Work Experience
I worked at [ABC Design Firm] last summer as an intern, where I had the opportunity to assist in [landscape mapping].去年夏天，我在 ABC 设计事务所担任实习生，在那里我有机会协助景观绘图。
This experience allowed me to apply my knowledge in a practical setting and enhanced my skills in communication / collaboration / problem-solving / [any other competencies].这次经历让我能够在实际环境中运用所学知识，并提升了我在沟通、协作和解决问题方面的能力。
Relevant Hobbies / Extracurricular Activities
In my leisure time, I enjoy [sketching nature], which helps me stay inspired and informed about the latest trends.在闲暇时间，我喜欢写生，这有助于我保持灵感并了解最新趋势。

Thank you for considering my application.感谢您考虑我的申请。
I look forward to the possibility of working together and contributing to your team.我期待着合作并为您的团队做出贡献的可能性。

Category 1 – Self-Introduction
类别一 – 自我介绍
1. Can you please introduce yourself in 2 minutes?
1. 你能用2分钟介绍一下自己吗？
Fill in: your name, nickname, diploma subject, company name & job position for part-time work, skills gained, and a strength adjective.
填写：你的姓名、昵称、文凭专业、公司名称及兼职职位、获得的技能、以及一个描述优势的形容词。
Your full introduction:
你的完整自我介绍：
Sample answer: Hi, my name is Sharon, you can call me Sherry. I graduated from IVE with a Higher Diploma in Landscape Architecture this summer. I have worked at ABC Design Firm as a part-time intern, where I had to assist with landscape mapping and site visits. I have gained better communication and time management skills. I am hardworking and responsible. I am confident that I will be a suitable person for this job.
Hi, my name is ___, you can call me ___. I graduated from IVE with a Higher Diploma in ___ this summer. I have worked at ___ as a part-time ___, where I had to ___. I have gained better ___ skills. I am ___. I am confident that I will be a suitable person for this job.
2. What do you do in your spare time?
2. 你业余时间做什么？
Choose an activity that shows relevance to the job opening. I 选择一个与职位相关的活动。
In my spare time, I like to ___ to relax. I often explore new ___ that could enhance my skills.
Example: In my spare time, I like to paint and draw to relax. I often explore new painting methods that could enhance my skills.
3. I see you have done some voluntary work. What did you learn from that?
3. 我看到你做过一些志愿工作。你从中学到了什么？
At my volunteering, I learnt to ___ (e.g. work with others / communicate with team members / manage time when doing projects).
Example: At my volunteering, I learnt to work with others, communicate with my team members, and manage time when doing projects.
4. What three keywords would your peers use to describe you?
4. 你的同学/同事会用哪三个关键词来形容你？
They would say I am ___ because I ___. They would say I am ___ because I ___. They would say I have ___ because I ___.
Example: They would say I am hard-working and responsible, because I often work overtime to complete all my tasks. Or, they would say I have a good sense of responsibility, because I often work overtime to complete all my tasks.

Category 2 – Strengths & Weaknesses
类别二 – 优势与劣势
1. Can you describe a time when your strengths helped you succeed?
1. 你能描述一次你的优势帮助你取得成功的经历吗？
There's a time when my ___ helped me succeed in ___. I was responsible for ___. By ___, the result was ___, and we received positive feedback.
Example: There's a time when my attention to detail helped me succeed in a project. I was responsible for checking the report before it was submitted. I noticed some small errors that could have caused problems. By fixing those mistakes, the result was the report was much clearer, and we received positive feedback.
2. What is a weakness you've identified in yourself, and how are you addressing it?
2. 你认识到自己的哪些不足，你是如何改进的？
Sometimes I might find it challenging to ___. I'm working on improving my ___. I have started ___. This has helped me ___.
Example: Sometimes I might find it challenging to meet tight deadlines. I'm working on improving my time management. I have started using project management apps and making daily to-do lists. This has helped me manage my schedule better.
3. What are the important qualities a person should have to become an effective team member?
3. 一个人需要具备哪些重要素质才能成为有效的团队成员？
To be an effective team member, a person should have ___. This helps ___. They should also be ___, so ___. Finally, being ___ is important, as it helps ___.
Example: To be an effective team member, a person should have good communication skills. This helps everyone understand each other. They should also be reliable, so others can depend on them to complete tasks. Finally, being open to feedback is important, as it helps improve the team's performance.
4. How do you ensure your weaknesses do not impact your work performance?
4. 你如何确保自己的不足不会影响工作表现？
I regularly review my tasks to identify any weaknesses. For example, if I struggle with ___, I ___. I also ask for feedback to improve. By being aware and taking action, I make sure my weaknesses do not affect my performance.
Example: I regularly review my tasks to identify any weaknesses. For example, if I struggle with public speaking, I practice my presentations in front of friends. I also ask for feedback to improve. By being aware and taking action, I make sure my weaknesses do not affect my performance.
5. In what areas do you feel you need the most improvement?
5. 你觉得自己在哪些方面最需要改进？
I feel I need the most improvement in ___. Sometimes I find it hard to ___. To work on this, I ___. This way, I hope to ___.
Example: I feel I need the most improvement in my networking skills. Sometimes I find it hard to connect with new people. To work on this, I am attending networking events and practicing my conversation skills. This way, I hope to build better relationships in my professional life.

Category 3 – Long / Short-term Goals
类别三 – 长期/短期目标
1. What is your plan for your personal growth and development?
1. 你的个人成长和发展计划是什么？
My plan includes ___. I aim to take online courses related to ___, seek feedback from colleagues, and attend relevant workshops. I believe continuous learning will help me grow personally and professionally.
Example: My plan includes setting clear goals for my skills and knowledge. I aim to take online courses related to my field, seek feedback from colleagues, and attend relevant workshops. I believe continuous learning will help me grow personally and professionally.
2. What are your long-term career goals?
2. 你的长期职业目标是什么？
My long-term goal would be to advance in my career to take on leadership roles, continuously improve my skills, and make a positive impact in ___.
Example: My long-term goal would be advancing in my career to take on leadership roles, also continuously improving my skills, as well as make a positive impact in my field and help others grow along the way.
3. What are your goals for the next 5 / 10 years?
3. 你未来5/10年的目标是什么？
5-Year Goal / 五年目标：In the next five years, I aim to deepen my expertise in ___ and take on more responsibilities, possibly in a leadership position.
Example (5-year): In the next five years, I aim to deepen my expertise in my field and take on more responsibilities, possibly in a leadership position.
Example (10-year): In ten years, I hope to be in a senior role where I can influence strategy and mentor others.
Example (Plan): To achieve these goals, I plan to pursue relevant certifications, seek mentorship, and focus on building a strong professional network.
10-Year Goal / 十年目标：In ten years, I hope to be in a senior role where I can influence strategy and mentor others.
Plan to Achieve Goals / 实现目标的计划：To achieve these goals, I plan to pursue relevant certifications, seek mentorship, and focus on building a strong professional network.
4. What would you like to study in the near future?
4. 近期你想学习什么？
In the near future, I would like to study ___. I believe this knowledge will enhance my abilities and help me contribute more effectively to my team and the company.
Example: In the near future, I would like to study video-editing. I believe this knowledge will enhance my abilities and help me contribute more effectively to my team and the company.
5. Will you consider taking any training courses related to this field / job?
5. 你会考虑参加与该领域/工作相关的培训课程吗？
Positive. I am definitely open to taking relevant training. I believe that ongoing education is important for keeping up with industry trends and upgrading my skills.
Example: Positive. I am definitely open to taking relevant training. I believe that ongoing education is important for keeping up with industry trends and upgrading my skills.

Category 4 – Problem-Solving
类别四 – 解决问题
1. How do you deal with difficult customers / irresponsible colleagues?
1. 你如何应对难缠的客户/不负责任的同事？
When dealing with difficult customers, I stay calm and listen to their concerns. I would also try to understand their point of view and find a solution together. For irresponsible colleagues, I communicate openly with them about the issues and suggest ways to improve our teamwork, focusing on positive outcomes.
Example: When dealing with difficult customers, I stay calm and listen to their concerns. What's more, I would try to understand their point of view and find a solution together.
For irresponsible colleagues, I communicate openly with them about the issues. In addition, I suggest ways to improve our teamwork, focusing on positive outcomes.
2. How do you manage multiple tasks?
2. 你如何管理多项任务？
To manage multiple tasks, I prioritise them based on deadlines and importance. I use ___ to stay organised and focused. I also ___, which makes it easier to complete them.
Example: To manage multiple tasks, I prioritise them based on deadlines and importance. I use a planner to write down what needs to be done daily. This helps me stay organised and focused. I also set small goals for each task, which makes it easier to complete them.
3. How do you handle office gossip?
3. 你如何处理办公室流言？
When I hear office gossip, I try to avoid participating in it. Instead, I focus on my work and encourage others to do the same. If the gossip affects my team, I may talk to a manager to address the issue professionally.
Example: When I hear office gossip, I try to avoid participating in it. Instead, I focus on my work and encourage others to do the same. If the gossip affects my team, I may talk to a manager to address the issue professionally. Keeping a positive environment is important for everyone.
4. Describe a difficult work situation / project and how you overcame it.
4. 描述一个困难的工作情况/项目，以及你是如何克服的。
In a previous project, we faced ___. I organised a meeting to discuss the challenges with my team. We divided the work into smaller tasks and supported each other. By staying focused and working together, we completed the project on time and met our goals.
Example: In a previous project, we faced a tight deadline with limited resources. I organised a meeting to discuss the challenges with my team. We divided the work into smaller tasks and supported each other. By staying focused and working together, we completed the project on time and met our goals.
5. Can you tell us about a situation where you analysed and solved a complicated problem?
5. 你能告诉我们一个你分析并解决复杂问题的情况吗？
Once, I had to analyse ___. I collected data and identified that ___. Then I proposed ___. After implementing it, ___, showing the importance of good analysis.
Example: Once, I had to analyse a drop in sales for our product. I collected data and identified that customers were confused about its features. Then I proposed a new marketing strategy with clearer information. After implementing it, sales improved significantly, showing the importance of good analysis.
6. How do you deal with conflicts with peers?
6. 你如何处理与同事之间的冲突？
When I have a conflict with a peer, I would first try to understand their perspective. I then approach them calmly and discuss the issue openly. If needed, I would involve a manager to help us resolve the conflict professionally.
Example: When I have a conflict with a peer, I would first try to understand their perspective. I then approach them calmly and discuss the issue openly, hoping to talk about our differences and find the common ground. If needed, I would involve a manager to help us resolve the conflict professionally.

Category 5 – Soft Skills & Work Experience
类别五 – 软技能与工作经验
1. Do you work better as a team member or alone? Why?
1. 你作为团队成员还是独自工作效果更好？为什么？
I prefer to work ___ because I ___. But I don't mind working ___ as well.
Example: I prefer to work as a team member because I like to hear other people's opinions as well. But I don't mind working alone.
Or: I prefer to work alone so I can focus better on details. But I don't mind working in a team as well.
2. Can you provide an example of your ability to work independently?
2. 你能举一个你独立工作能力的例子吗？
When I was ___, I had to ___. At the end, I was able to finish before the deadline.
Example: When I was studying, I had to work on our individual projects and come up with a business plan / design concept / an app design, where I spent hours researching and working on it. At the end, I was able to finish before the deadline.
3. How do you handle stress and pressure?
3. 你如何应对压力？
I like to ___, as it helps me relax and recharge.
Example: I like to play basketball, as it helps me relax.
4. Do you mind traveling frequently to Mainland China?
4. 你介意经常前往中国内地出差吗？
No, I don't mind at all. I am happy to be able to see how things work in China as well.
Example: No, I don't mind at all. I am happy to be able to see how things work in China as well.
5. Are you willing to work under pressure and overtime?
5. 你愿意在压力下工作和加班吗？
Yes, I don't mind working overtime to ___ (e.g. get things done / meet project deadlines).
Example: Yes, I don't mind working overtime to get things done.
6. Do you have any full-time / part-time work experience?
6. 你有全职/兼职工作经验吗？
Yes, I have been working at ___ as a/an ___, where I was responsible for ___.
Example: Yes, I have been working at 7-11 as a part-time salesperson, where I was responsible for the cash register and the overall organisation of the store.
7. What have you gained from your summer work / part-time work?
7. 你从暑期工作/兼职工作中收获了什么？
I am able to ___ better when I worked as a ___ at ___.
Example: I am able to communicate better / work better with others / manage time better when I worked as a salesperson at 7-11.
8. What do you know about the type of work we expect from you?
8. 你对我们期望你完成的工作类型了解多少？
Since it is an entry level position, I would expect to ___ (e.g. help colleagues with anything they need assistance with, learn the internal processes, and contribute to projects).
Example: Since it is an entry level position, I would expect to be helping colleagues with anything they need assistance with.
